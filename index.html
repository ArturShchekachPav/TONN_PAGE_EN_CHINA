About us

DonGguan Ziyu Checking Fixture Co. Ltd. is located in DongGuan city, south China. It focuses on the design and manufacture of checking gauges and fixtures for automobile parts, which is completely implemented in accordance with ISO management system. The company adheres to the quality policy of "people-oriented, quality-oriented, efficient and punctual, and strives for perfection", and provides the best quality products and services for domestic and foreign auto parts manufacturers. In addition, it pays special attention to the reserve and training of talents, and has more than 10 senior engineers with over 6 years' experience, who use UG, CATIA, AutoCAD and other high-end software to design, and 100% meet the needs of customers in all aspects.
